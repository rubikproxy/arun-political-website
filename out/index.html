Our Vision for a Just India

Rooted in a legacy of service, the Indian National Congress is committed to building an equitable and prosperous future for every citizen.

“

The well-being and advancement of the people of India and the establishment in India, by peaceful and constitutional means, of a Socialist State based on Parliamentary Democracy in which there is equality of opportunity and of political, economic, and social rights and which aims at world peace and fellowship.

”

Founding Principles

Socialism

Advocates for a Socialist State based on parliamentary democracy, emphasizing social equity, economic justice, and equitable distribution of resources.

Secularism

Promotes religious neutrality, ensuring equal treatment and respect for all religious communities in governance and public life.

Democracy

Upholds parliamentary democracy with a commitment to equal freedom, non-violent governance, and active citizen participation.

Nationalism

Focuses on fostering national unity, preserving India's sovereignty, and promoting a collective Indian identity while respecting diversity.

Progressivism

Emphasizes forward-thinking policies that promote social, economic, and environmental reforms to address contemporary challenges.
